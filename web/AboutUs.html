About Us

Anyone Can Make a Difference

Here at RakCity (bitch) we aim to make it easier for people to help other people. After all, I bleed red, and so do other humans in this world. Actually, everyone does because that is what makes us fundamentally human. So, getting back to the topic, our application allows users to earn virtual currency based on good deeds and their GPS location. If they are near enough to a "good deed site", either a soup kitchen, food bank, hobo, or vagrant, they can check in and earn these points which can be redeemed for real life prizes like Amazon gift cards and domain.com domain names. These scores will be placed on a leaderboard for the whole world to see! This leaderboard gets over 3 billion hits every month, so there is a huge incentive for people to get their names out there.
This app was featured in the New York Times, 3rd article issue 3 on November 18, 2017. You can buy it from your local newsstand for $3.95 and we will get 5%, which we will donate to charity.
Special thanks to HackWestern and the wonderful creators Kevin, Andrew, Brock for building this revolutionary piece of software that today, changes Western. Tomorrow, changes the world.
Because truly anyone can make a difference, and have fun doing so.
